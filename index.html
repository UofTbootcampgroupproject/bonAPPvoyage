    How to use the App

    1. Pick meal type and cook time
    2. Click on the Search button
    3. Scroll on the list of Recipes
    4. Select a recipe
    5. You will see Points of interest on a particular city of that country
    6. Click on the View Recipe button
    7. Follow the recipe steps
    8. Click on Full Recipe Button for addition details
